About SMCC-CCIS Week 2025

SMCC-CCIS Week 2025 is a yearly celebration highlighting the talents and skills of Computer Studies students. The event showcases technology competitions, seminars, exhibits, digital arts, and fun activities.

Event Highlights ★ ★

  • 🏫 TechTalks with Guest Speakers
  • 💻 Hackathon & Coding Battles
  • 🎮 E-Sports ML Tournament
  • 🏆 IT Capstone & Research Exhibit
  • 🎭 Opening Parade & Talent Showcase

Event Schedule 📅

Date: February 10-14, 2025

Venue: SMCC Campus Grounds

Various activities are scheduled throughout the week covering IT, computer science, gaming, and tech innovation.
